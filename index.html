Focaccia
Ingredients
• 1 teaspoon instant yeast
• 1 tablespoon salt
• 1 1/2 cups water
• 3 1/4 cups All-Purpose Flour
• olive oil
• whatever you want for seasoning!
(I like rosemary)
Steps
• Combine flour, salt, yeast, and water in a
large bowl
• Once mixed, cover bowl and set aside to
rise for 8-24 hours
• One cup at a time, add the flour
*Preheat the oven to 400°F
• Place on a small sheet pan
• Add dimples!
• Add toppings!
• Bake until golden brown
Final step!
Basic Loaf
Ingredients
• 1 tablespoon active dry yeast
• 1 tablespoon sugar
• 1 tablespoon salt
• 2 cups warm water not over 110°F
• 5 1/2 to 6 cups All-Purpose Flour
• Flour for dusting
Steps
* Preheat over to 375°F
• In a large bowl, mix the yeast, sugar,
salt and water. Let stand until yeast is
dissolved
• One cup at a time, add the flour
• Knead the dough on a floured surface
for about 5 minutes
• Grease the mixing bowl with olive oil
• Knead again for 2-3 minutes
• Let it rise until doubled in size
• Place in 2 greased 9x5 in loaf pans
and score it
• Bake for about 30-35 minutes or
until it turns golden brown
Final step!
Sweet Rolls
Ingredients
• 2 1/4 tablespoon instant yeast
• 1/4 cup honey
• 1/2 teaspoon salt
• 1/4 cup water
• 1/2 cup milk or milk substitute
• 2 tablespoons salted butter or
butter substitute
• 2 1/4 cups All-Purpose Flour

Topping:
•1/4 cup salted butter
•1 1/2 tablespoons honey
Steps
• In a large bowl, combine 1 cup flour,
yeast and salt. Stir until blended.
• Combine milk, water, butter, and honey
and microwave in 30 second intervals
until warm to the touch. Add flour to mix-
ture gradually until dough forms into a ball.
• Knead for 6-8 minutes.
• Cover with a towel and let sit for 10 minutes
• Cut the dough in 12 pieces and shape into
balls
*Preheat oven to 375°F
• Place in pan to bake, let rise for 30 minutes
• Bake for 20 minutes
• Combine ingredients for topping, then
brush atop the rolls!
Final step!
